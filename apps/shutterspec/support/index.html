Common questions

  • Where is my data stored? Your data is stored locally on your device.
  • How do backups work? You choose where to save backups using the iOS share sheet or available storage providers.
  • How do exports work? You can export and share opening sheets or job PDFs using the app’s export and share features.
  • Can I attach photos? Yes. ShutterSpec lets you attach inside photos, outside photos, and sketch photos to each opening.
  • Do I need an account? No. ShutterSpec does not require account registration or login.
