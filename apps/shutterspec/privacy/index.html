Privacy Policy

ShutterSpec respects your privacy and is designed to work without collecting personal information.

ShutterSpec stores customer, job, shutter opening, measurement, specification, photo, and note data locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to ShutterSpec servers.

If you choose to use backup, export, or sharing features, you decide where your data is saved or shared (for example, iCloud Drive, Files, email, AirDrop, or other storage and sharing providers available through the iOS share sheet). ShutterSpec does not have access to these files once exported or shared.

ShutterSpec does not require account registration or user login.

ShutterSpec may request access to your camera and photo library so you can capture and attach site photos, opening photos, sketches, and other job-related images. These images are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

ShutterSpec may request access to notifications in order to provide local reminders related to jobs, measurements, follow-up tasks, or other app features. Notification permissions can be changed or disabled at any time in your device settings.

If ShutterSpec includes optional premium features or subscriptions, purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. ShutterSpec may use RevenueCat solely to manage purchase and entitlement status within the app. This process may involve the use of an anonymous app-specific identifier and purchase history for the purpose of enabling premium features. This information is not linked to your identity and is not used for tracking.

ShutterSpec does not use advertising services, does not display ads, and does not use tracking technologies.

ShutterSpec does not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties.
