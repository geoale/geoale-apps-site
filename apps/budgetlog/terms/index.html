BudgetLog offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions.

Subscription

BudgetLog Pro is offered as an auto-renewable subscription with the following options:

  • Monthly subscription
  • Yearly subscription

Subscription prices are displayed within the app and may vary by region, currency, and applicable taxes. The user will always be shown the price and terms before completing the purchase.

Subscriptions may include a 7-day free trial for eligible users. Payment will be charged to the user’s Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase (or at the end of the free trial, where applicable).

The subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period (or free trial). The account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period, and the cost of the renewal will be identified. The user can manage and cancel subscriptions in their Apple ID account settings in the App Store at any time after purchase.

Any unused portion of a free trial period (if offered) will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription.

The subscription benefits may include premium BudgetLog features available only to subscribed users.
