Privacy Policy

BudgetLog respects your privacy and is designed to work without collecting personal information.

BudgetLog stores budget, income, expense, and transaction data locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to BudgetLog servers.

If you choose to use backup or export features, you decide where your data is saved (for example, iCloud Drive or other storage providers available through the iOS share sheet). BudgetLog does not have access to these files once exported.

BudgetLog does not require account registration or user login.

Subscriptions are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. BudgetLog uses RevenueCat solely to manage subscription status (entitlements) within the app. This process may involve the use of an anonymous app-specific identifier and purchase history for the purpose of enabling subscription features. This information is not linked to your identity and is not used for tracking.

BudgetLog does not use analytics services, advertising services, or tracking technologies, and does not display ads.

BudgetLog may send local notifications for reminders related to budgets, payments, or other app features. Notification permissions can be changed or disabled at any time in your device settings.

BudgetLog does not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties.
